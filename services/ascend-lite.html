Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Ascend and Ascend Lite?

Ascend is the full marketing engine — website, content, ads, AI tools, brand identity, and strategy. Ascend Lite focuses on lead generation infrastructure: sales funnel, paid ads, strategy, analytics, and brand foundation. Lite is the starting point. Ascend is the complete operation.

Can I upgrade to Ascend later?

Yes. Ascend Lite is built to graduate into Ascend. When you're ready, we waive the integration fee and everything we built carries forward. No wasted work.

Do I own everything?

Yes. Your sales funnel, ad accounts, analytics, and brand assets are all built on your accounts and your domain. If you leave, you take everything with you.

What's the contract?

Month-to-month contract. No long-term lock-in. Give us 30 days notice and you keep everything we built. We don't hold your business hostage.

What results can I expect?

Most Lite clients see qualified leads within the first 30 days of launch. Your sales funnel and paid ads are built to convert from day one, with ongoing optimization to improve cost-per-lead over time.
