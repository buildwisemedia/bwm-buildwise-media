Under the Hood

The 12 Stages

Every stage has a clear deliverable. Nothing moves forward until the previous stage is done right.

Phase 1 — Discovery (Week 1)

01 Brand Capture
A 30-minute onboarding call where we learn who you are, what you do, and what makes your business different. We capture your voice, your values, and your competitive edge — straight from you.
02 Research & Strategy
We dig into your market, your competitors, and your ideal customers. Keywords, search trends, ad landscape, local competition — we map it all so your engine targets the right people from day one.
03 Brand Voice Card
Your brand's tone, language, and personality — locked into a single document. Every piece of content, every ad, every page sounds like your business. Not a template. Not generic. Yours.
04 Content Brief
The blueprint for everything we're going to create. Website pages, ad copy, blog topics, social content — all mapped out before a single word is written. Strategy first, production second.
05 Brand Proof
Your checkpoint. We show you the brand direction, sample content, and strategy before anything goes live. If it doesn't feel right, we fix it here. Nothing launches without your sign-off.

Phase 2 — Build (Weeks 2–4)

06 Website Build
A fast, conversion-focused website built on your domain. SEO-ready from the start. Mobile-first. Designed to turn visitors into calls — not just look pretty.
07 Content Production
Blog posts, service pages, location pages, social content — all written in your brand voice. AI-powered production with human quality gates. Volume without sacrificing quality.
08 AI Tools Setup
Automated follow-ups, review requests, lead notifications, and workflow tools — set up on your accounts, in your name. The systems that keep the engine running while you're on a job site.
09 Ad Campaign Launch
Google Ads, Meta Ads, or both — targeted to your service area and ideal customers. Built from real search data, not guesswork. Your ad accounts, your data, your leads.
10 Analytics & Tracking
Call tracking, form tracking, conversion pixels, dashboards — everything wired up so you know exactly where every lead came from. No guessing. No vanity metrics.
11 Go-Live QA
Before anything goes live, we run a full quality check — speed, mobile, SEO, tracking, forms, links. Every detail gets tested. Your engine launches clean.

Phase 3 — Run (Ongoing)

12 Ongoing Optimization
Your engine doesn't just run — it gets better. We adjust ads, add content, improve conversions, and compound your results month over month. Monthly reports keep you in the loop without burying you in data.

FAQ

Common Questions About the Process

How long until my marketing engine is live?
Your full marketing engine — website, content, ads, AI tools — is built and live within 30 days. Discovery takes about a week. The build takes two to three weeks. Then we flip the switch.
What do I need to provide?
A 30-minute onboarding call, access to your existing accounts (domain, Google Business, ad accounts), and honest answers about your business. That's it. We handle the rest.
What if I don't like the Brand Proof?
The Brand Proof is your checkpoint. If something doesn't feel right, we revise it before a single page goes live. Nothing launches without your approval.
How much of my time does this take on an ongoing basis?
After launch, the engine runs itself. You answer the phone when leads call. That's your only job. We handle optimization, content, ads, and reporting.
Do you provide monthly reviews?
Yes. Every month you get a clear report — what's working, what we're adjusting, and where the numbers are heading. No jargon. No vanity metrics. Just the numbers that matter to your business.
